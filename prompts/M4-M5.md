## Part A — M4: Coached Mode (FeltSide)

Re-read docs/specs/DEALING.md in full first. It is the spec; this list
is the checklist.

1. **Coached table lifecycle.** Lesson-synced scheduled tables (stored
   since M3) become joinable at lesson time and activate as coached
   tables; coach can also open ad-hoc coached tables from the lobby.
   Soft seat restriction from the lesson's student list, coach override
   from the table (PRD §7). Coach seated-as-player or observing — both.
2. **Dealing panel** (coach sidebar only): per-slot type-ahead
   (DEALING §2.1 grammar exactly), 52-card grid, range-draw toggle;
   partial specification with origin computed
   (rng|manual|hybrid|scenario); card integrity enforced centrally.
3. **Street-by-street dealing** through the card-source seam built in
   M2: `awaiting_deal` state, action timers suspended, players see a
   neutral "dealer is acting" state, one-key RNG escape hatch,
   pre-staging allowed.
4. **Repetition:** Re-deal (exact cards repeat; range slots re-draw);
   Save-as-scenario snapshots the full panel config incl. street
   policy.
5. **Range matrix editor — BUILD NEW** (decision 0004): 13×13 grid,
   drag-select, range-string import/export via the graduated
   RangeParser. legacy/extracted/* is raw reference material only.
   This component is shared with Part B's chart editors — build it
   once, reusable.
6. **Visibility rules** (PRD §3.1, non-negotiable): hole cards always
   closed in shared UI; coach sidebar shows ONLY coach-assigned
   cards/ranges; pure-RNG cards hidden from the coach even when seated.
   Enforce server-side in per-socket payloads, never client-side.
7. **Coach controls:** pause/resume, undo last action, street rollback,
   stack adjust, blind change, force street, award pot. Undone actions
   are marked, never silently erased; chip conservation asserted after
   every control.
8. **Live coach tagging** mid-play without pausing → tag_type `coach`
   in recording/export.
9. **Scenarios & playlists:** ONE schema generation (PRD §4). Scenario
   = panel config; playlist = ordered scenarios; activation runs drills
   in order with per-drill re-deal. The §4.6 playlists export goes
   real; a lesson's playlist_id preloads at activation.
